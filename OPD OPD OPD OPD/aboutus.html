ABOUT US

Our team understands the struggles that students face when it comes to finding affordable and accessible learning resources. We believe that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background. That's why we have created OPEN PDF DOWNLOAD, a website that provides free books for students from class 1 to class 10.
Our goal is to disrupt the traditional book publishing industry and make education more accessible and convenient for students. We believe that by providing free and open access to educational materials, we can help students to learn and grow in their academic pursuits.
As a team, we are passionate about education and believe that everyone should have access to quality learning resources. Our team is made up of individuals who are driven to make a positive impact on the education industry and are committed to improving the way people learn.
Our admin is a student just like you, studying at Sunway College in Kathmandu which is affiliated with Birmingham City University and are committed to providing quality educational resources to students across the globe. We invite you to join us in our mission to disrupt the education industry and make learning more accessible and affordable for everyone.
